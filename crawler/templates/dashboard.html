IDENTIFYING TRENDS IN PROGRAMMING LANGUAGES, FRAMEWORKS AND TECHNOLOGIES

Social media is the fastest way to analyze what’s trending. Reddit and Tumblr are widely used discussion platforms. To understand the trends in programming languages, frameworks, and technologies, hot topics in relevant domains, we are collecting data and analyzing it.
